Shipping and Delivery Policy

This policy details the delivery process for our digital products. Since PHARMACY CRM is a digital specialized software, we do not ship physical goods.

1. Delivery Method

Our products are delivered digitally via the email address provided during the checkout process. Upon successful payment, you will receive an automated email containing your download links and license activation keys.

2. Delivery Timeline

Delivery is instant. Once your payment is confirmed by the payment gateway (PhonePe), our system triggers the delivery email immediately. You should receive it within seconds to a few minutes.

3. What If I Don't Receive the Email?

If you haven't received your delivery email within 10 minutes, please follow these steps:

  • Check your Spam or Junk folder.
  • Verify that the email address you entered during checkout is correct.
  • If you still can't find it, contact our support team at support@pharmacycrm.example.com with your transaction details.

4. Shipping Costs

Being a 100% digital product, there are no shipping charges, handling fees, or physical logistics costs involved.

5. Serviceable Areas

Our digital products are available globally. As long as you have an internet connection and a valid payment method, you can purchase and receive PHARMACY CRM anywhere in the world.
