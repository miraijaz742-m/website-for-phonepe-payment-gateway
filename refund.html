Refund and Cancellation Policy

Thank you for choosing PHARMACY CRM. Please read our policy regarding refunds and cancellations for our digital products.

1. Nature of Product

PHARMACY CRM is a digital software product. Due to the nature of digital goods, which are delivered instantly and cannot be physically "returned," we generally do not offer refunds once the license key has been delivered to your email.

2. Refund Eligibility

We only consider refund requests under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If you experience a critical technical issue that our support team cannot resolve within 7 business days.
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same product twice.
  • Non-delivery: If the automated delivery system fails and we are unable to provide the product manually within 48 hours.

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please email support@pharmacycrm.example.com within 7 days of your purchase with your order ID and the reason for the request. We will review your request and notify you of the approval or rejection within 3-5 business days.

4. Cancellations

Since the product is delivered instantly upon payment, orders cannot be cancelled once the payment process is completed. Please ensure you review the product details and system requirements before making a purchase.

5. Processing Refunds

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment (e.g., via PhonePe) within 7-10 business days.

Contact Us

For any questions related to refunds, please contact us at support@pharmacycrm.example.com.
